Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert kilograms to pounds?

To convert kilograms to pounds, multiply the weight in kilograms by 2.20462. For example, 10 kg = 22.0462 pounds.

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the temperature by 9/5 and add 32. The formula is: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

What is the difference between KB and KiB?

KB (kilobyte) is 1,000 bytes, while KiB (kibibyte) is 1,024 bytes. KB uses the decimal system (powers of 10), while KiB uses the binary system (powers of 2).

How accurate are the conversions on this website?

Our conversions use standard conversion factors and provide results with high precision. For scientific or critical applications, we recommend verifying with specialized equipment.
